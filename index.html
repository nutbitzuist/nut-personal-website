// About

The System Behind the Systems

I've spent 15+ years navigating the financial industry — from institutional trading desks to building my own algorithmic trading systems. What started as a career in investment and trading evolved into a deep obsession with systems thinking and the power of leverage.

For the past 6+ years, I've dedicated my evenings and weekends to building and optimizing Expert Advisors (trading robots) that generate additional income and cashflow. This journey has taught me that the real edge isn't in any single trade — it's in building systems that compound over time.

Today, I apply systems thinking to everything: trading strategies, business operations, and personal development. I believe in leveraging technology, capital, and processes to multiply output while minimizing manual effort — that's the 100x philosophy.
